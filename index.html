How is UCOLLEX different?

UCOLLEX is an innovative platform that sells unique and limited edition NFTs for toy and
pop culture collectibles. Founded by artists who are also collectors themselves, we want
to offer NFTs in an accessible way to everyone. A place where collectors can feel at
home and live their passion for collecting through exclusive content, interviews with
artists, the best 3D art, and innovative VR experiences.

What is digital art?

An NFT is a digital asset that represents real-world objects like art, music, in-game
items, videos and are becoming an increasingly popular way to buy and sell digital
artwork. They are bought and sold online, frequently with cryptocurrency, and are
generally encoded with the same underlying software as many cryptocurrencies.

Can I pay with my credit card?

We want as many individuals to enjoy the wonderful of digital art as possible so
UCOLLEX lets you purchase digital art using various Fiat Payment Methods and allows
users to choose the corresponding ones based on their currencies or region. You can
buy your first NFT with your credit card.
